Our History

Founded in 2010, TechCon began as a small regional event and has since grown into one of the largest international technology conferences, attracting thousands of professionals, students, and innovators each year.

Over the years, the conference has featured groundbreaking product announcements, insightful talks, and a thriving community of tech enthusiasts.

Our Mission

TechCon is committed to fostering innovation, building connections, and shaping the future of technology. We believe in empowering individuals through shared knowledge, hands-on experiences, and cross-industry collaboration.
